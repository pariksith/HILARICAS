About Us

The Festival

Where talent meets opportunity, and creativity knows no bounds

Experience the Magic of HILARICAS 2025

HILARICAS 2025 is the flagship annual cultural festival organized by H.I College of Arts & Science, Coimbatore. A grand celebration of talent, creativity, and youthful exuberance that brings together students from across Tamil Nadu.

With over 30 competitive events spanning music, dance, drama, fine arts, literary arts, and digital media, HILARICAS offers a platform for students to showcase their exceptional talents and compete for glory.

Join us for a day filled with electrifying performances, fierce competition, cultural diversity, and unforgettable memories. This is where future stars are born and legends are made.
